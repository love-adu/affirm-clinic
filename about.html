Our Mission

We are an undergraduate-run clinic providing **information support** for name and gender marker changes. We help gather requirements, explain documents, and support community members in building plans for legal affirmation.

Confidentiality Agreement

AFFIRM Clinic @ Berkeley maintains strict confidentiality. Information shared with student volunteers will not be disclosed outside the clinic except with explicit permission or when legally required.
